The map below depicts in blue the New Mexico portion Continental divide trail and associated forst service lands (in Red), wilderness lands (in green), roadless lands (purple, black outline) , and National Landscape Conservation System land (pink) that the trail may navigate through. Some land features are on top of one another, and opacity allows the user to see the compunded layers of land use. The purpose of this map is to find some of the most remote areas of New Mexico visited by the Continental divide trail.
